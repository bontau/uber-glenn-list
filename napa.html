Glenn's Recommendations

Napa Valley Dining Guide

Restaurant and brunch picks across Downtown Napa, Yountville, St. Helena, and Calistoga.

Page 1

Restaurants in Downtown Napa

  • Slanted Door (Vietnamese) - a new addition to Napa from famous chef Charles.
  • ZuZu (Tapas) - Spanish tapas with a California twist.
  • Torc (Farm to Table) - a high energy fun atmosphere with delicious worldly eats.
  • Amami (Japanese) - locals favorite sushi spot.
  • Scala Osteria (Italian) - offers traditional Southern Italian, seafood-centered recipes.
  • Allegria (Italian) - Italian with California flare. Historic bank building with vault still intact.
  • Don Giovanni (Italian) - a rustic warm Italian ambiance serving fresh Italian and French style cuisine.
  • Morimoto (Japanese) - inventive, high-end Japanese fare & artful cocktails.
  • Cole’s Chop House (Steak House) - a classic American steakhouse specializing in all cuts of meat.
  • Kenzo (Japanese) - a upscale Japanese Michelin restaurant with a prix fixe sushi & kaiseki menu.
  • Eiko’s (Japanese) - family owned restaurant serving modern Japanese cuisine, and original sushi rolls.
  • Norman Rose Tavern (American) - the menu offers elevated takes on American cuisine and cocktails with upward of 40 wines available.
  • Bounty Hunter (BBQ) - casual brick-walled establishment serving BBQ classics, and craft brew.
  • Waterfront Grill (Seafood) - located right on the Napa River serving up the best locally-sourced seafood.
  • Morimoto Napa (Asian) - on chef Morimoto’s pan Asian restaurant.
  • Entrecot (Argentinian) - Argentine cuisine authentic new concept to enjoy a variety of tapas, and steaks.

Brunch in Downtown Napa

  • Grace’s Table - a global kitchen focusing on France, Italy, and the Americas.
  • Boon Fly Cafe - casual Californian dining with a lively vibe & modern roadhouse feel.
  • Jax Diner - airy low-key American diner providing burgers, salads and breakfast including beignets.
  • Model Bakery - featuring artisanal breads pastries & home to the famous Model Muffin.
  • Winstons Bakery - this community-focused cafe and bakery features bagels, shmears, sandwiches, and salads.
  • Monday Bakery - a woman-owned bakery with a rotating seasonal menu of savory & sweet treats.

Restaurants in Yountville

  • Bottega (Italian) - Michelin star rustic Italian fare produced with the finest local ingredients.
  • Bouchon (French) - modern French cuisine in addition to a raw bar.
  • Bistro Jeanty (French) - local favorite for over 20 years serving classic homestyle French cuisine.
  • R + D Kitchen (American) - polished upscale with a dress code policy out by elevated meat & seafood options.
  • Restoration Hardware (California) - guests dine in the indoor/outdoor space amidst trickling fountains.
  • French Laundry (French) - three-Michelin-starred Napa Valley destination serving a daily 9 course meal.
  • North Block Restaurant (Thai Fusion) - features regionally farmed, fished and foraged Northern California fare.
  • La Calenda (Mexican) - features dishes from Oaxaca while casting a glance across a region of Mexican dishes.
  • Ciccio (Italian) - featuring elements from their nearby farm the menu boasts salads and wood fired pizzas.
  • Brix (American) - long-running spot for farm to table California fare set in sprawling vineyards.
  • Ottimo (Italian) - a foodie’s paradise offering gourmet food, wood-fired pizzas, wine, paninis, and more.
  • Mustards Grill (California) - a Napa landmark for over 30 years with an onsite organic garden.

Restaurants in St. Helena

  • Press (Steak house) - the Press Culinary Team draws inspiration from around the globe.
  • Brasswood Bar + Kitchen (American) - a gathering place for locals and visitors alike.
  • Farmstead (American) - showcasing ingredients fresh from their farms with a seasonally changing menu.
  • Rutherford Grill (American) - a local favorite with a neighborhood feel, featuring American comfort food.
  • Goose & Gander (American) - amazing rustic American food, craft cocktails and wine in rustic digs with a patio.
  • Market (American) - seasonal American comfort fare served in a cozy bistro with rustic stone walls.
  • Charter Oak (California) - centered on a family-style dining experience with decadent dishes.
  • Cook (Italian) - petite eatery draws locals with its locally sourced Northern Italian cuisine.
  • Pizzeria Tra Vigne (Italian) - enjoy dining at a locals casual spot with Northern Italian cuisine.
  • Gillwoods Cafe (Brunch) - casual spot offering classic American homestyle dishes.
  • Harvest Table (California) - an honest and refined California bistro using local ingredients in high-end dishes.
  • Auberge du Soleil (Mediterranean) - Napa Valley’s first fine dining restaurant.
  • Charlie’s - a casual family run community focused restaurant serving American food.

Restaurants in Calistoga

  • Sam’s Social Club - is the place to meet for food that is seasonal, creative and thoughtfully prepared.
  • Calistoga Brewery - its 150 seat patio & beer garden is nestled along the Napa River and there’s live music.
  • Evangeline - garden patio dining available at a beautiful French bistro with a Creole soul.
  • Lovina - the meal and experience makes one feel like they’re at a good friend’s house eating comfort food.
  • Buster’s - Buster fills the streets with the whispering aroma of authentic Louisiana barbecue.
  • Sushi Mambo - a fun and relaxed atmosphere, a great selection of beer, wine, sake and shochu.
  • Johnny’s - located in mount view hotel spa offering many TVs for entertainment brews and great food.
  • Cafe Sarafornia - with its famous breakfast served all day it’s a long-time locals favorite.
  • Veraison (Farm to Table) - a French inspired farm to table spot with a focus on local sustainable.
  • Solbar - divides its menu into light, delicate flavors to stimulate your palate, and bold, savory dishes.
  • Pacifico - serves authentic and regional Mexican cuisine specializing in moles, tamales, fajitas and more.
  • Sam’s General Store - gourmet coffee pastries and unique items from Napa Valley.
  • La Prima - signature pizza, Italian and Mediterranean specialties, pasta couroner, appetizers, and salads.
